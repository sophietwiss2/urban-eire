What is Bound Apparel?

Activewear That Goes Way Beyond the Gym

Dublin-born Abbie Beggs started the Irish sportswear company Bound Apparel in 2020. Abbie started Bound when she was barely 20 years old and about to enter her last year of college. She had trouble finding sportswear that was attractive, high-quality, and reasonably priced (Bound Apparel, 2020).

The company soon gained recognition for its “not just for the gym” apparel, a concept that underlies every item they produce. Four-way stretch fabric is used in their best-selling Boundless collection to provide comfortable, premium sportswear that looks great in the gym as well as on campus or in a café.

The brand has a loyal community of students who love the quality, the fit and the Irish-made ethos behind every piece. From leggings and sports bras to tracksuits and shorts, Bound Apparel empowers women to feel confident and stylish in every aspect of their lives — whether that’s a morning workout, a college lecture or a night out in Dublin (Bound Apparel, 2023).

College students who adore each product, quality, and Irish-made craftsmanship make up the brand’s sizable fan market.

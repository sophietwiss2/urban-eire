About

One Platform. Two Irish Brands. Endless Style.

UrbanEire is an Irish fashion platform built to celebrate the most popular Irish brands that college students adore in Ireland. The top two choices this week are two independent Irish brands, Ditsy Bits and Bound Apparel, both of which feature similar and stylish items that are perfect for college students worldwide, but especially for Ireland (Bound Apparel, 2023; DITSY BITS, 2024). Urban Éire introduces you to these popular and trending websites and explores in detail the hot picks you need for your college wardrobe. Urban Éire makes it easy for students like us to discover and shop pieces that are genuinely designed with Irish women in mind. From Dublin-made Guinness graphic tees to high-quality activewear, UrbanÉire is where Irish culture meets contemporary style.

As a college student, finding fashion that balances personality, budget, and everyday practicality can be a challenge — especially when most options are faceless international fast-fashion chains. Urban Éire changes that. Whether you are after the iconic Slainte Guinness tee from Ditsy Bits for a night out, or a Bound Apparel leggings-and-sports-bra set that takes you from the gym to the campus cafe without missing a beat, this platform puts everything in one place. Browse both brands, compare styles, and find your fit, all while supporting independent Irish designers who actually get student life. Enjoy!

Bound Apparel

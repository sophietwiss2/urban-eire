What is Ditsy Bits?

Wearing Irish Culture Has Never Looked This Good

Ditsy Bits is an independent Dublin-based SME, founded by Emilie Williams in 2022 (DITSY BITS LIMITED, 2022). Ditsy is known for celebrating Irish culture through its designs, primarily screen-printed t-shirts made from 100% organic cotton and limited capsule drops and emerging knitwear (DITSY BITS LIMITED, 2022).

Emilie was a student at DCU who started the firm from her bedroom as a hobby while selling at weekend markets (EVOKE, 2025). The company grew into a studio with 3 staff members, where she made a strategic choice to outsource manufacturing in Guangzhou as demand increased (Wrenne, 2024; Walsh, 2025; Irish Times, n.d.).

Ditsy Bits’ online presence is centred around an e-commerce site (ditsybits.com) supported by high-engagement social channels such as Instagram and TikTok that showcase short-form content, behind-the-scenes of production and product reveals that drive traffic and in-person pop-up events (Ditsy Bits, n.d.; RSVP Live, n.d.).

Young college students who appreciate distinctive, locally inspired designs and sustainable materials make up the majority of Ditsy Bits’ primary target. From tracksuits to iconic tees, Ditsy Bits has it all set up and ready from lectures to nights out!!
